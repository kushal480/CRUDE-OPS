⚠ GEOPOLITICAL CRUDE RISK ZONES
🛢 Strait of Hormuz
CRITICAL
Iran-US tensions ongoing. 20% of global crude + 17% LNG transit daily. Any naval incident = $15–30/bbl instant spike. IRGC harassment of tankers continues.
🇮🇳 India: ~40% of crude via Hormuz. BPCL/IOC/HPCL most exposed. Nifty Energy sells off hard on any escalation.
⚠ Bab el-Mandeb / Yemen
CRITICAL
Active Houthi missile & drone attacks on commercial vessels. Suez Canal traffic down ~50%. Most major tankers now rerouting via Cape of Good Hope (+10–14 days).
🇮🇳 India: Tanker day-rates +300%. India importers pay $2–4/bbl extra freight. Red Sea war risk insurance premium spiked 10x.
⛵ Suez Canal / Red Sea
HIGH
Houthi threat forces Cape of Good Hope diversion for majority of VLCC/Suezmax traffic. Egyptian canal revenue collapsed.
🇮🇳 India: Europe-India route now +14 days. WTI-Brent spread widens. West Africa crude freight to India rises sharply.
⚓ Black Sea — Russia
HIGH
Russia-Ukraine war ongoing. Novorossiysk terminal exports ~1.5 mb/d. G7 price cap on Russian crude at $60/bbl. Shadow fleet operating.
🇮🇳 India: Urals crude at $12–15 discount vs Brent. India imported ~35–40% from Russia in 2024. Any escalation risks this cheap supply.
🚢 Strait of Malacca
MEDIUM
80% of China and Japan crude imports transit here. Piracy risk. Congestion near Singapore anchorage. Alternative via Lombok Strait.
🇮🇳 India: Asian crude premium surge if disrupted. East coast refinery (Chennai, Vizag) costs rise. SGX crude futures spike.
⛽ Persian Gulf (OPEC core)
HIGH
Iraq, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Iran — all major loading terminals. OPEC+ production cut decisions originate here. Saudi Aramco OSP set monthly.
🇮🇳 India: Saudi OSP changes directly hit BPCL/IOC refinery margins. Kuwait/Iraq crude = primary source for Indian refineries.
